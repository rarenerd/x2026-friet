## Underlying composition — "Freed from Desire"

"Freed from Desire" (1996) is composed by Gala Rizzatto, Maurizio
Molella ("Molella"), and Filippo Carmeni ("Phil Jay"). Published by
**Do It Yourself Music Records** and other labels. **All rights reserved
by the rights-holders.**

This project is a **transformative fan/educational remix** that:

- Studies the source song's harmonic structure (key, chord progression,
  rhythmic patterns) for didactic purposes — see `docs/song_analysis.md`
  and `docs/melody_analysis.md` for the music-theory analysis.
- Re-arranges those structural elements onto the C64 SID chip's
  three-voice limitation, producing chiptune output that is
  significantly different in instrumentation, tempo, dynamics, and
  presentation from the original recording.
- Does not redistribute the original recording or its MIDI/sample stems
  outside of this repository's `midi/` folder, which contains karaoke
  MIDIs that were already publicly available on free MIDI-archive sites.

The karaoke MIDIs in `midi/` are themselves transcriptions whose
copyright belongs to their (uncredited) creators. They are included
under fair use for research and personal-study purposes; they are not
the original copyrighted recording.

If you are a rights-holder and object to any material in this
repository, please open an issue at
`https://github.com/annejan/friet-met-desire/issues` and the offending
material will be removed.
